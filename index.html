Longevity

Data exploration

This study uses a well known database of 10058 men recruited in 1963 and were followed up for 5 years to investigate ischemic heart disease incidence (Medalie et al. 1973). The goal is to predict longevity using mid-life predictors.

Whats inside?

This data frame contains 136 variables:

  1. Demographics data (32)

  2. Medical data including medical history, laboratory tests and measurements (29)

  3. physical (4)

  4. work (32)

  5. family (11)

  6. diet (23)

  7. Outcome - reaching to the age of 95, prevalence of 6.9 %

What are we going to do

  1. prepare our data for ML analysis

  2. create specifications for the different models

  3. hypertune parameters

  4. examine results
